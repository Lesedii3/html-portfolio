The Best Movies According to Lesedi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Sarafina

A powerful film set in apartheid-era South Africa,
Following a young black girl named Sarafina as she becomes
involved in the Soweto Uprising and fights against oppression.
It reminds me of my countrys history

Grown-Ups

This a comedy film about a group of childhood friends,
who reunite after many years for a Fourth of July weekend at a lake house.
The movie follows their hilarious antics and heartwarming moments,
as they reminisce about the past and navigate the challenges of adulthood.
I love it because it's a feel-good movie

Pink Panther

classic comedy film following the clumsy but endearing French detective,
Inspector Jacques Clouseau, as he tries to solve the mystery of the stolen Pink Panther diamond. Filled with slapstick humor and clever wit, it's a timeless comedy that keeps audiences entertained with its hilarious antics and memorable characters
